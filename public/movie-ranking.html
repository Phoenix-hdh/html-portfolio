The Best Movies According to Me- Nitul

My top 3 movies of all time

Your Name (Kimi no nawa)

Makoto Shinkai created a masterpiece of modern romance anime. The story of Mitsuha Miyamizu from a small village and Taki, a high school boy living in the heart of Tokyo. Their lives takes a huge turn when they collide in an unexpected way possible.

Interstellar

The greatest Sci-fi movie of all time. This movie resonates with every person that loves space exploration.

Spirited Away

A classic masterpiece directed and created by Hayao Miyazaki. The beautiful visuals and the storytelling is out of the ordinary.
